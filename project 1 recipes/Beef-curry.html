Beef Curry Recipe

Ingredients

  • Beef (chuck or stewing) 1 kg (cut into cubes)
  • Onion 2 large (finely chopped)
  • Garlic 4 cloves (minced)
  • Ginger 1 tablespoon (grated)
  • Tomatoes 2 large (chopped)
  • Coconut milk 400 ml
  • Beef broth 250 ml
  • Vegetable oil 2 tablespoons
  • Curry powder 2 tablespoons
  • Garam masala 1 tablespoon
  • Cumin 1 teaspoon
  • Coriander powder 1 teaspoon
  • Turmeric 1 teaspoon
  • Salt to taste
  • Pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro (for garnish) a handful

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a large pot or Dutch oven over medium-high heat.
  2. Add the beef cubes and cook until browned on all sides, about 5-7 minutes.
  3. Remove the beef and set aside.
  4. In the same pot, add the onions and cook until softened and golden brown, about 5 minutes.
  5. Add garlic and ginger, cooking for another minute until fragrant.
  6. Add curry powder, garam masala, cumin, coriander powder, and turmeric. Stir well to combine with the onions.
  7. Return the beef to the pot along with any accumulated juices.
  8. Add tomatoes and cook for 2-3 minutes until they start to break down.
  9. Pour in coconut milk and beef broth. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to low and simmer covered for 1.5-2 hours or until the beef is tender.
  10. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Garnish with fresh cilantro before serving.

Tips

  • For a richer flavor, use a combination of coconut milk and beef broth.
  • Ensure the beef is well-browned before adding the vegetables to enhance the overall taste.
  • Simmering the curry for a longer time allows the flavors to meld together more effectively.
  • Add fresh cilantro just before serving to preserve its vibrant flavor and color.
  • Adjust the spice levels according to your preference by adding more or less curry powder and garam masala.

Enjoy your delicious Beef Curry!
